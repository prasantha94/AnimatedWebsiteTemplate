Animation website

Animation is not just for cartoons anymore. From full-screen moving
images to small hover effects, touches of animation are popping up
everywhere. Animation is trendy, fun, and user-friendly. And the
obstacles to using animation have started to fall. With most users on
high-speed connections and the ease of creating anything from simple
movements or a silly gif to several minutes of action, animations have
become practical and useful web design tools.
